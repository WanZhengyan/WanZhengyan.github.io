Research
Error Analysis of Discrete Flow with Generator Matching
Zhengyan Wan, Yidong Ouyang, Qiang Yao, Liyan Xie, Fang Fang, Hongyuan Zha, Guang Cheng

arxiv

We derive the KL divergence of two path measures regarding two continuous-time Markov chains (CTMCs) with different transition rates by developing a novel Girsanov-type theorem, and provide a comprehensive analysis that encompasses the error arising from transition rate estimation and early stopping.

Discrete Guidance Matching: Exact Guidance for Discrete Flow Matching
Zhengyan Wan*, Yidong Ouyang*, Liyan Xie, Fang Fang, Hongyuan Zha, Guang Cheng (*: Equal Contribution)

arxiv code

We derive the exact transition rate for the desired distribution given a learned discrete flow matching model, leading to guidance that only requires a single forward pass in each sampling step, significantly improving sampling efficiency.

High-Dimensional Model Averaging via Cross-Validation
Zhengyan Wan, Fang Fang, Binyan Jiang

arxiv code

We propose a high-dimensional model averaging method under a general framework, and establish rigorous theoretical guarantees for the proposed estimator.
